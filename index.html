Suber

Web Development | Full-Stack Development

An app built for Fall Hacks 2023 using NextJS, TailWindCSS, Typescript, Javascript and Google Maps API. It is student uber-like app where it allows students to car-pool with other students to reduce carbon emissions while building a stronger sense of community. Students can make a posting when they are going to drive to school and other students can see these postings and book a ride with them in the same direction.
